# Global Retail Expansion & Revenue Analysis

🔗 **[View the Interactive Tableau Dashboard Here](https://public.tableau.com/shared/R57WQRKWG?:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link)**

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing an online retail dataset to uncover insights into revenue trends, high-value customers, and global expansion opportunities. The final deliverable includes an interactive Tableau dashboard tailored to answer specific strategic questions for the CEO and CMO.

## 🧹 Data Preparation & Due Diligence
To ensure the analysis was accurate and error-free, a thorough data cleaning process was executed prior to visualization:
* **Removed Returns:** Filtered out all transactions with negative quantities.
* **Pricing Accuracy:** Removed records where unit prices were below $0.

## 📊 Strategic Questions Addressed
This dashboard provides actionable business context rather than just a read-out of numbers, addressing four key areas:
1. **Revenue Forecasting (CEO):** Analyzed 2011 monthly revenue trends to identify seasonal peaks and aid in future forecasting.
2. **Geographic Performance (CMO):** Identified the top 10 revenue-generating countries (excluding the UK) and compared revenue against sheer volume.
3. **Customer Retention (CMO):** Highlighted the top 10 highest-value customers based on revenue to support targeted VIP retention campaigns.
4. **Global Expansion (CEO):** Mapped global product demand (units sold) to pinpoint regions with the highest organic demand outside the primary market.
